About TEVTA

Technical Education & Vocational Training Authority

Empowering Punjab's youth through quality technical education and vocational training since 1999.

Our Mission

To develop a quality workforce through technical and vocational training that meets national and international labor market demands, fostering economic growth and social development in Pakistan.

Our Vision

To be the leading technical and vocational training institution in Pakistan, recognized globally for excellence in workforce development and technical education.

  • Modern Technical Education Programs
  • Industry-Driven Vocational Training
  • Skilled Workforce Development
  • Professional Certification Courses
  • Industry Linkages and Partnerships
